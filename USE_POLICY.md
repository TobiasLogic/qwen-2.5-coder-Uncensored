you agree to the following.

- Writing, reviewing, debugging, and learning software across general
  programming, web development, and systems work.
- Application-security work: finding and fixing vulnerabilities in code and
  systems **you own or are explicitly authorized to test**.
- Security tooling for **authorized** engagements, lab environments, CTFs, and

## Prohibited uses

You may not use this model or pipeline to:

- Attack, scan, or test systems you do not own and are not authorized to test.

  infrastructure, self-propagating worms, or persistence/rootkit tooling.
- Conduct fraud, harassment, stalking, or unauthorized surveillance.
- Do anything illegal under the laws that apply to you.
